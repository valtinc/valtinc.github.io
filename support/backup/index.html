Backing up your Valt

Backup works by storing your encrypted Valt in the Valt cloud. Don’t worry, it’s not only guarded by your images, but also by your Valt Secret, which is like a booster shot of security.

Backup allows Valt users with a single device to recover their Valt in case of a lost, stolen or broken device. Users who are syncing can often recover their Valt more easily by re-authorizing the device

Backup works by registering your device through email and then writing down your Valt secret on a sheet of paper. Your Valt secret is composed of 12 words and follows the BIP-0039 Standard (BIP is a great acronym, right?) You can always find your Valt secret by going to Settings and selecting Recovery Code and Valt secret. Here’s what it looks like.

Instructions for backup

To back up your Valt on iOS or Mac Desktop, select the Backup and syncing option in Valt Preferences and enable the option. You will be asked for your email address. Confirm the email by clicking on the link.

If this is not the first device you are authorizing, then Enabling backup and syncing will cause this Valt to synchronize

Instructions for restore

  1. To restore your Valt from backup using your Valt secret, select Restore using my Valt secret on the Valt splash screen (you can also restore a Valt from the Incorrect Image Selection screen).

  2. Enter your email address to authenticate this device for restoration.

  3. In your email, confirm by clicking the link.

  4. Valt will download your encrypted Valt from the Cloud and prompt you to enter your Valt secret.

  5. After entering your secret, you’ll be free to unlock your Valt.

Examples

  1. Restore your Valt after mistakenly deleting the App Suppose your friend Huma accidently deletes your Valt app. You download Valt again from the App Store and on the splash screen tap Restore using my Valt secret. After entering your email and confirming, you grab your Valt secret from underneath your mattress and type in the 12 words. Then you select your Images and your Valt is restored.

  2. Restore your Valt when you don’t have easy access to another device Suppose you’re syncing your Valt on your iPhone and your Mac but you left your iPhone at your mother-in-law’s house in Rogers, Arkansas. Your friend Xing deletes your Valt app mistakenly from your Mac. You download Valt for Mac from the App Store. Normally you would just re-authorize your Mac because you’re syncing, but unfortunately you don’t have your iPhone. In this case, you can restore using the example from above.
