iOS App

We support iPhones, iPads, and iPod touches running iOS >10.0.

  1. Visit Valt’s App Store page on your iOS device.
  2. Select GET, and enter your Apple ID password.

Mac App

We support all Apple computers running macOS >12.2 (all versions on or after Sierra).

  1. Visit Valt’s App Store page on your computer.
  2. Select GET, and enter your Apple ID password.

Safari Browser Extension

The Safari Browser Extension is installed automatically when you download the macOS app. Open Safari on your computer and verify that the Valt icon appears to the left of the search bar. If not, follow these steps to enable, disable, or uninstall the extension on your Mac.

Google Chrome Browser Extension

We support all versions of Google Chrome for your Mac computer. Before beginning, first install the Mac app

  1. Visit Valt’s page on the Chrome Web Store from your Google Chrome browser.
  2. Select Add to Chrome. Review the privacy information popup, and click Add Extension.
  3. If the Valt icon does not appear automatically just right of the search bar, learn how to enable the Google Chrome browser extension.
  4. Learn more about the browser extension.
