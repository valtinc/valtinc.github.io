Valt Synchronization and Backup

Valt Synchronization works by initally registering a device through email and then authorizing subsequent devices through clients that have been previously authorized.

Please note, it is important to initiate syncing using the device on which you created your Valt.

  1. To enable Valt syncing on your iOS or Mac Desktop, either click on the Enable backup and syncing banner in the inventory or select the Enable backup and syncing option in Valt preferences.

  2. You will be asked for your email address.

    a. If this is the first device you are authorizing, an email will be sent to that address. You must confirm this email by clicking on the Confirm email link.

    b. If this is not the first device you are authorizing, then an authoriziation request will be sent to all devices you have previously authorized. You must confirm the request on one of your authorized devices.

Example: register iPhone and then synchronize with Valt for Mac

Suppose you created your Valt on your iPhone. You can register this Valt by enabling syncing and backup in the Settings and then confirming over email. Now you can download Valt for Mac. When launching for the first time, you can choose to use an existing Valt, which will pompt you for your email address. Because you already have a Valt registered under this address, Valt will send an authorization request to your iPhone. Confirming this request on your phone will synchronize your iPhone Valt and your Mac Valt.
