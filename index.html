Valt | Visual Password Manager
A new approach to password management. All your passwords, there when you need them, safe when you don’t.

Simple

Your master password consists of images in place of a traditional and often complex password.

Safe

Your images encode a computer-generated random master for a high-level of security.

Secure

Your data is protected by AES 256-bit encryption using salted passwords, PBKDF2 key-stretching, and a random 128-bit device authentification key.
